About MintonShardeum

Your gateway to creating custom tokens on the Shardeum blockchain

What is MintonShardeum?

MintonShardeum is a user-friendly platform that allows anyone to create their own ERC-20 tokens on the Shardeum blockchain without any coding knowledge.

Features

  • Easy token creation in minutes
  • Full ERC-20 standard compliance
  • Customizable token parameters
  • Low deployment fees
  • Support for both Mainnet and Testnet

How It Works

  1. Connect your MetaMask wallet
  2. Choose your network (Mainnet or Testnet)
  3. Fill in your token details
  4. Pay the deployment fee (5,000 SHM)
  5. Confirm the transaction
  6. Your token is deployed!

Why Shardeum?

Shardeum is a scalable, secure, and low-cost blockchain perfect for deploying tokens. With linear scalability and low fees, it's ideal for both developers and users.
